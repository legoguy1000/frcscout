Join Team

To join your team, please follow the steps below.
  1. Click the login button at the top right corner
  2. Login with one of the providers (Google, Facebook, Microsoft Live)
  3. Select the user icon in the top right corner an select "My Profile"
  4. In the "Team Information" panel, search for your team by name or number
  5. Select your team from the drop down and verify that the inforamtion presented matches
  6. Select Join team (If your team account has not been created yet, you will be prompted to register your team)
  7. Your team admin will be contacted to approve your request
  8. Once your request is approved, you have joined your team

Register Team

  1. Follow steps 1-5 of "Join team"
  2. After selecting your team, you should be presented with the option to "Register Team"
  3. After clicking "Register team", you will become the team admin
  4. Approve or deny future team join requests

Scout Match

  1. Select The Season
  2. Select "Match Data Entry"
  3. If you have a current event selected, that event will be selected with the next match to be scouted)
  4. If there is no current event, select the event and match from teh dropdowns
  5. When the match starts, select the "Start Match!" button. This will set the start time and will sync accross all of your scouts
  6. Select a team from the list
  7. Select the actions as they occur
  8. All data is synced accross scouts, so multiple people scouting a single team will see eachothers data
  9. You may change the team you are scouting at any time. Upon changing teams, you will see any data already scouted for that team
