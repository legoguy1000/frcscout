Join Team

To join your team, please follow the steps below.
    Click the login button at the top right corner
    Login with one of the providers (Google, Facebook, Microsoft Live)
    Select the user icon in the top right corner an select "My Profile"
    In the "Team Information" panel, search for your team by name or number
    Select your team from the drop down and verify that the inforamtion presented matches
    Select Join team (If your team account has not been created yet, you will be prompted to register your team)
    Your team admin will be contacted to approve your request
    Once your request is approved, you have joined your team
